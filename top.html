Co-Founders

Prof. Boon Tech Ooi

Boon Teck Ooi was born in Malaysia. He received the B. Eng. (Honors) from the University of Adelaide, Australia, the S.M. from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) and the Ph.D. from McGill University, Montreal, Canada. He is presently the life fellow of IEEE, life professor in McGill University, and the visiting professor in Singapore Nanyang Technological University and Malaysia University. He is the pioneer and founder of VSC-HVDC theory, the international renowned expert on FACTS, multi-terminal HVDC and wind generation.

Dr. Bin Lu

Bin Lu received the B. Eng. from Tsinghua University, the M.Eng. and the Ph.D. from McGill University. His research interests include FACTS nonlinear control, HVDC control & protection system, DMS and in-memory database system for real time application, micro-grids, energy internet, etc.
